Festivals and Events

People form different provences live in the town. People from different states live like a family and celebrate different festival and appriciate culture of each other. From time to time different Festival are celebrated with harmony.

Art & craft

Rajhara is very diverse in Terms of art and crafts. Art and Craft events are organised from time to time for children. During the summer holidays children are very active in this events.Rangoli Drawing Painting Drama Singing Dancing Sports Are some of the events children actively participate and show their talents.

Flower Show Festivel of Spring

Each Year Flower Show is organised in February mont of the year.People gether together in Saptgiri Garden and celebrate this cultural fest together.Different cultural and artistic talent are showcased in exhibition and events.Varity of colorful spring flowers and Vasu's beautiful rangoli are special attraction of the festivel.

Dussehra Festival

Dussehra is celebrated by the town's people each year in Rajhara's Footbal Ground. Whole town gather to celebrate and witness this colourful festival.
